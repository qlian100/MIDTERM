ABOUT ME

My name is QIQI LIANG. I am from Guangzhou China, but I moved to NYC five years ago. I went to High School, John Dewey High School in Brooklyn, NY. I am currently a Junior and majoring in IT in Syracuse University. I like taking pictures. I love NYC because there are so many beautiful places that I can take pictures with, such as Botanic Garden, Central Park, Bryan Park, and graffiti art walls. I am also a food junkie. I love NYC because it is such a big mixed culture city that I can try all kinds of food from different countries at one time.
